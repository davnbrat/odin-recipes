Banana Smoothie

Description

A recipe so easy the kids can make it for themselves. With ingredients you likely already have in your kitchen. You can adjust this to add other ingredients such as peanut butter, cocoa powder, whip cream on top. You can also remove the sugar for a healthier version.

    Ingredients

  • 2 Bananas
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • 1 Cup Ice
  • 3 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • Optional: 1 Cup Yogurt

    Directions

  1. Pour Vanilla Extract in a blender
  2. Add Bananas
  3. Add Yogurt
  4. Add Ice
  5. Blend until smooth
